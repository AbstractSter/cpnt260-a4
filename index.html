The Smith Contemporary Art Gallery

Established in 2023, The Smith Contemporary Art Gallery (SCAG) is located in Calgary, AB, Canada.

The SCAG's goal is to elevate the work of Canadian artists from coast to coast to coast up to an international level. This is done through larger scale shows that feature artists from diverse backgrounds.
